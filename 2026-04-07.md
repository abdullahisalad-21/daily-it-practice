### 4.3 Major Cyberattacks
- **WannaCry (2017)**: ransomware worm using EternalBlue SMB exploit.  
- **ILOVEYOU (2000)**: VBScript email worm using social engineering.

### 4.4 Antimalware Protection & Malware Removal
Learned the full workflow:
1. Gather & verify symptoms  
2. Monitor processes  
3. Quarantine device (disconnect network, disable backups)  
4. Run offline malware scan  
5. Update threat definitions  
6. Remove malware  
7. Re-enable backups & create clean restore point  
8. Educate users

# 6. What I Learned 📚
- Security revolves around understanding how threats exploit vulnerabilities to create risk.  
- Malware categories differ by propagation method, payload, and stealth.  
- WannaCry demonstrated the danger of unpatched systems; ILOVEYOU showed the power of social engineering.  
- Proper malware removal requires isolation, offline scanning, and updated definitions.  
- User education is the strongest long-term defense.
