# 📚 6. What I Learned  
- Domain controllers authenticate both the *computer* and the *user*, not just the user  
- Group Policy controls Windows Hello availability  
- Virtual machines cannot use Windows Hello Face due to missing IR hardware  
- FSMO roles are essential for AD stability and conflict‑free operations  
- A second domain controller is required for redundancy; one DC cannot back itself up  
- Enterprise login logic includes password rules, time sync, GPO restrictions, and group membership  
- Sysadmins control who can join devices, who can log in, and what authentication methods are allowed
