# 📚 6. What I Learned (Technical Takeaways)
- WEP is broken due to RC4 + 24‑bit IV reuse  
- WPA2 uses AES‑CCMP for strong confidentiality and integrity  
- The 4‑way handshake derives unique PTKs per client  
- WPS PIN validation is fundamentally insecure  
- Packet sniffing requires promiscuous mode (wired) or monitor mode (wireless)  
- Tcpdump is ideal for CLI capture; Wireshark is ideal for deep analysis  
- Proxies protect clients; reverse proxies protect servers
